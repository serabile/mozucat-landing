Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mozucat ("we", "our", "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Mozucat handles your information when you use our IPTV streaming application.

Mozucat is a media player application that allows you to connect to your own IPTV service providers. We do not provide, host, or distribute any media content.

2. Information We Collect

Mozucat collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Server Credentials: The server URL, username, and password you provide to connect to your IPTV service. These are stored securely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Viewing Preferences: Your favorites, playback history, and continue watching progress to enhance your experience.
  • App Settings: Language preference, display settings, and other configuration choices you make within the app.
  • Cache Data: Temporarily cached content metadata (channel names, movie information, cover images) to improve loading performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Connect to your IPTV service provider on your behalf
  • Display your content catalog and channel listings
  • Track your viewing progress for resume playback
  • Maintain your favorites and personalized lists
  • Improve app performance through local caching

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. Mozucat does not operate any cloud servers or remote databases for user data.

  • Credentials are stored in encrypted local storage
  • No personal data is transmitted to Mozucat servers
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected by the app
  • Cache data can be cleared at any time from your device settings

Please note that when you connect to your IPTV service, network requests are made directly between your device and your service provider's servers. Mozucat has no control over how your service provider handles your data.

5. Third-Party Services

Mozucat interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Your IPTV Service Provider: The server you configure handles your streams and content catalog. Their privacy policy governs how they process your connection data.
  • Content Delivery Networks: Cover images and metadata may be loaded from external sources as provided by your IPTV service.

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to any third party.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • Clear cache: Clear cached content data from the app settings.
  • Manage favorites: Add or remove favorites at any time.
  • Clear history: Remove your viewing history and progress.
  • Disconnect: Log out to remove stored credentials.

7. Children's Privacy

Mozucat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The content available through the app depends entirely on the user's IPTV service provider, and parental supervision is recommended.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Push Notifications and Communications

Mozucat uses push notification services (Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android, Apple Push Notification Service on iOS) to deliver notifications to your device.

What is collected

  • A unique device push token, generated by your operating system, used solely to deliver notifications to your device
  • No personal information, viewing history, or IPTV credentials are sent to these services

How notifications are used

  • Service-related events (downloads finished, playback events, app updates)
  • Important security or account-related alerts
  • Optional announcements about new features

Your control

  • You can disable all push notifications at any time from your device's system settings
  • Disabling notifications does not affect any other functionality of the App

These third-party services handle message delivery according to their own privacy policies. We do not share viewing data or credentials with them.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:
