How it works

Three steps, that's it.

Mozucat plugs into your existing IPTV subscription and elevates it.

1

Download Mozucat

From the App Store or Google Play. Instant setup, no hidden configuration.

2

Connect your IPTV subscription

Enter your M3U playlist or Xtream Codes credentials. Mozucat handles the rest: sorting, metadata, EPG.

3

Enjoy a refined experience

Live TV, movies, series — it's all there, beautifully presented, played without friction.

Mozucat is an IPTV client. You bring your own subscription (M3U / Xtream). The app provides no content.
